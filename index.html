About

From the workshop floor to the browser

Before screens, I worked with my hands — welding, car mechanics, construction, phone repair. Every one of those trades taught the same lesson: find what's actually broken, fix it, and make sure it holds. I bring that same instinct to digital marketing — less guesswork, more diagnosis.

I lead with strategy: social media management, content planning, and outreach that's built around how people actually decide to buy. Where it helps, I go a step further and build the website, booking flow, or dashboard the strategy depends on — so clients get one person who understands the whole picture, not two vendors handing off a brief.
